A well-known educational institution, Anshu Memorial Academy in Rajapakar, Vaishali was founded in the year 2017. This Primary Schools takes pride in the holistic and innovative learning methods which have helped them raise the bar in the field of education. The centre follows a well-researched curriculum that is based on the play way method of learning. This school offers education for children in different age groups. There are a host of other supplementary activities that children can enroll in to enhance their skill set. In a populous suburb of vaishali, this centre is situated near Bhatha Chowk of Rajapakar. Undoubtedly it is one of the best Primary Schools in Rajapakar, Vaishali. Infrastructure and other Facilities at Anshu Memorial Academy Anshu Memorial Academy in Rajapakar has an environment which is not only child-friendly but engaging for children to learn and have fun at the same time. It has spacious classrooms that are well appointed with various amenities and facilities. This centre employs caring and involved certified teachers that play an instrumental role in the learning and development of the kids.
